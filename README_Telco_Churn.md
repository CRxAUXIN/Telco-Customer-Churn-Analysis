## Key Analyses Performed

- High-risk customer identification by churn probability
- Approximate CLTV and top customers by LTV
- Revenue loss estimation from churned customers
- Segmentation & KPI generation for retention planning

## Business Recommendations
- Offer retention incentives to high-risk, high-LTV customers
- Convert month-to-month users to annual plans with offers
- Improve billing transparency to reduce churn among high-bill users
- Prioritize customers with multiple service complaints for proactive outreach
